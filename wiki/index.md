## Overview

PYATB (Python ab initio tight binding simulation package) is an open-source package for computing electronic structures and related properties from ab initio tight-binding Hamiltonians. Developed at USTC under Prof. Lixin He.

- **Repository:** https://github.com/pyatb/pyatb
- **Documentation:** https://pyatb.github.io/pyatb/
- **PyPI:** https://pypi.org/project/pyatb/ (v1.1.2)
- **Paper:** arXiv:2303.18004, CPC 291 (2023) 108844
- **License:** GPL-3.0
